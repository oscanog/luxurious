# Level 3 — Indicators & Oscillators

**Subtitle:** Junior
**Color:** hsl(43 96% 48%)
**Description:** Use RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, and multi-indicator strategies.

---

## Lesson 3.1 — RSI
**Duration:** 6 min

Relative Strength Index measures momentum on a 0-100 scale.

- **Above 70** = Overbought (might fall)
- **Below 30** = Oversold (might rise)
- **Divergence** = Price makes new high but RSI doesn't → reversal signal

Default period: 14. Don't change it unless you know why.

---

## Lesson 3.2 — MACD
**Duration:** 7 min

Moving Average Convergence Divergence. Two lines + histogram.

- **MACD Line** crosses above **Signal Line** → Buy signal
- **MACD Line** crosses below **Signal Line** → Sell signal
- **Histogram** shows the distance between the lines

Best used on 4H and Daily charts. Ignore on 1-minute charts.

---

## Lesson 3.3 — Bollinger Bands
**Duration:** 6 min

A volatility envelope around price:

- **Middle band** = 20 SMA
- **Upper band** = +2 standard deviations
- **Lower band** = -2 standard deviations

**Squeeze** = Bands tighten → Explosion incoming
**Walk the band** = Strong trend, price rides upper/lower band

---

## Lesson 3.4 — Stochastic Oscillator
**Duration:** 5 min

Similar to RSI but compares closing price to range:

- **%K** (fast) and **%D** (slow) lines
- Above 80 = Overbought, Below 20 = Oversold
- Crossovers generate signals

Best in ranging/sideways markets. Less useful in strong trends.

---

## Lesson 3.5 — Combining Indicators
**Duration:** 8 min

One indicator = guess. Multiple = conviction.

**Don't stack similar indicators.** RSI + Stochastic = redundant (both momentum).

**Good combos:**
- Trend (EMA) + Momentum (RSI) + Volume
- Bollinger Bands + MACD + Support/Resistance

**Rule of 3:** Enter only when 3+ factors align. This filters out 80% of bad trades.

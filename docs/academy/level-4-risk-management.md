## Lesson 4.1 — Position Sizing
**Duration:** 6 min

The #1 reason traders blow up: risking too much per trade.

**The 1% Rule:** Never risk more than 1% of your account on a single trade.
- $10,000 account → Max risk = $100 per trade
- This lets you survive 20+ losing trades in a row

**Formula:** Position Size = (Account × Risk%) / (Entry - Stop Loss)

---

## Lesson 4.2 — Stop-Loss Strategies
**Duration:** 7 min

A stop-loss is your seatbelt. Always wear it.

**Types:**
- **Fixed** — Set at a specific price level
- **Trailing** — Moves with price to lock in profits
- **ATR-based** — Uses volatility to set dynamic stops

**Never move your stop-loss further away.** That's hope, not strategy.

---

## Lesson 4.3 — Risk-Reward Ratio
**Duration:** 5 min

If you risk $1, how much can you make?

- **1:1** — Break even at 50% win rate. Not good enough.
- **1:2** — Profitable at 34% win rate. Good.
- **1:3** — Profitable at 25% win rate. Excellent.

**Rule:** Only take trades with minimum 1:2 R:R. This one rule will transform your results.

## Lesson 4.4 — Portfolio Diversification
**Duration:** 5 min

Don't put all eggs in one basket:

- Trade multiple uncorrelated assets
- BTC and ETH are correlated — that's NOT diversification
- Mix crypto + forex + commodities for true diversification

**Correlation trap:** During crashes, everything correlates. Keep cash reserves.

---

## Lesson 4.5 — The Trading Plan
**Duration:** 8 min

No plan = no edge. Your trading plan must include:

1. **What** you trade (assets, timeframes)
2. **When** you enter (setup criteria)
3. **Where** your stop-loss goes
4. **How much** you risk (position size)
5. **When** you take profit
6. **Review** schedule (daily/weekly journal)

Write it down. Follow it religiously. Update it monthly.
